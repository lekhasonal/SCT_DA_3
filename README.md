## Overview

The HR Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project developed to analyze employee turnover patterns and identify the key factors contributing to attrition within an organization. Employee attrition is a critical business challenge, as high turnover rates can impact productivity, increase recruitment costs, and affect overall organizational performance.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of employee attrition by transforming raw HR data into meaningful visual insights. Through interactive charts, KPIs, and filters, users can explore attrition trends across different departments, job roles, age groups, genders, overtime status, and job satisfaction levels.

## Objective

The primary objective of this project is to understand why employees leave the organization and identify the factors that have the greatest influence on attrition. The dashboard helps HR professionals and decision-makers make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

## Features

- Interactive Tableau Dashboard
- Attrition Rate KPI
- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Gender-wise Attrition Distribution
- Job Role vs Attrition Analysis
- Overtime Impact on Employee Turnover
- Job Satisfaction vs Attrition Analysis
- Dynamic Filters for Better Exploration
- User-Friendly Dashboard Design
- Data-Driven Workforce Insights

## Tools and Technologies

- Tableau
- HR Employee Attrition Dataset
- Data Visualization Techniques
- Business Intelligence Concepts

## Key Insights

- Employees working overtime tend to have higher attrition rates.
- Certain departments experience significantly higher employee turnover.
- Job satisfaction plays an important role in employee retention.
- Some job roles are more prone to attrition than others.
- Employee demographics such as age and gender influence attrition trends.

## Benefits

This dashboard enables organizations to monitor employee turnover patterns, identify high-risk areas, and implement effective retention strategies. By leveraging interactive visualizations and analytical insights, stakeholders can better understand workforce behavior and improve overall employee satisfaction.

## Conclusion

The HR Employee Attrition Dashboard demonstrates the power of data visualization in solving real-world business problems. By analyzing attrition trends and workforce metrics, the dashboard provides actionable insights that support informed decision-making and help organizations build a more stable and productive workforce.
